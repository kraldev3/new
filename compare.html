What changes

The pieces that come together

Each Shaderzone pass adds its own slice of cinematic quality, layered onto Roblox's existing render.

Lighting

Soft global illumination, accurate shadows, and natural sun light replace Roblox's flat lighting model.

Reflections

Water, glass and polished surfaces gain real-time screen-space reflections with proper Fresnel falloff.

Color & tone

Filmic tone mapping, expanded color gamut and contrast-aware adjustments. No more washed-out scenes.

Atmosphere

Volumetric fog, god rays and bloom give scenes the kind of mood you usually find in a screensaver of a film.
