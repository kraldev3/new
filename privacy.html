Privacy Policy

How we handle your data. Short version: we keep it minimal, and we never sell it.

Last updated: May 27, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Shaderzone ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website (shaderzone.org) and our desktop application.

By using Shaderzone, you agree to the practices described below. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

2. Information we collect

We only collect the minimum data needed to operate Shaderzone:

  • Account data (optional): email address if you create a Shaderzone Pro account.
  • Anonymous telemetry: GPU model, OS version, app version and crash logs. No personally identifying information is attached.
  • Cookies: the website uses essential cookies only (theme preference and session). No third-party advertising cookies.

What we do NOT collect: Roblox usernames, in-game data, screenshots, keystrokes, browsing history, or anything outside the Shaderzone application.

3. How we use information

  • To operate, maintain and improve the Shaderzone application.
  • To diagnose crashes and performance issues.
  • To send service-related communications (account verification, security alerts).
  • To respond to support requests you initiate.

We never use your data for advertising and we never sell it to third parties.

4. Data sharing

Your information is shared only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the service (hosting, error tracking). These providers are bound by data-processing agreements.
  • Legal authorities, only when compelled by a valid legal request.

5. Data retention

Anonymous telemetry is retained for up to 12 months. Crash logs are retained for up to 90 days. Account data is kept while your account is active and deleted within 30 days of account deletion.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of telemetry (Settings → Privacy → "Disable telemetry").
  • Export your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@shaderzone.org.

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Access to user data is logged and limited to engineering staff with a documented need.

8. Children's privacy

Shaderzone is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above and, for account holders, by sending an email.

10. Contact us

Questions or concerns about this policy? Reach us at privacy@shaderzone.org or through the contact page.
